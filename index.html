Having at a minimum, a warm coat and gloves while mountain climbing is extremely important. There are differetnt brands to choose from and there are even some that have built-in heat.

When choosing a pair of winter gloves for climbing, dexterity is important. The climber must be able to use his or her fingers to grip equipment or hangon to the surface of a rock.

Insulation and breathability stand out as probably the most important properties of hiking clothing. Insulation must ensure protection against the cold while allowing transmission of perspiration from the skin to the environment at the same time.

Making sure to wear layers that can be removed so overheating does not occur is important.
